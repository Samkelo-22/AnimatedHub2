About AnimatedHub

AnimatedHub is a start-up dedicated to showcasing original animations and teaching the craft through a community-based learning center.

Mission

To become the default platform that animators and fans produce and refer to, with a selection of original animations and a learning hub.

Vision

To democratise the learning process of animation and give a global stage to digital storytellers.

Target Audience

  • Aspiring animators (age 16–30)
  • Independent studios
  • Animation enthusiasts and educators
  • Students of digital arts and design
